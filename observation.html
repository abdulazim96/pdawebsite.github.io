What do I need to know about observation ?

Suitable For
Early-stage patient
Cost
  • Depends on hospital.
  • Cost is mainly for consultation and testing.

Active Surveillance

Procedure
  • Involves regular follow up with examinations and tests.
  • PSA and prostate examination will be done every few months and prostate biopsy every 1 to 3 years.
  • Active treatment such as surgery or radiotheraphy still remain as options in case of disease progression.
Suitable for
Patient who have small cancer confined to the prostate gland and it doesnt grow fast.

Watchful Waiting

Procedure
  • Involves less frequent follow up and examinations than active surveillance
  • Only done if there is concern that the cancer is growing too fast or patient develop symptom.
Suitable for
Patient who is old and have a lot of serious health problem.

Advantages

Can avoid complications of active treatment such as :
  • Trouble controlling bladder or bowels
  • Trouble having an erection
Can change your treatment option
  • You can always change your treatment option to either surgery or radiotheraphy.
Cheaper cost
  • Cost is cheaper compared to active treatment (surgery and radiotheraphy).But active surveillance is more costly than watchful waiting as it require more frequent testing and follow up.

Disadvantages

Feel worried or anxious
  • You may feel worried or anxious about living with cancer and putting off the treatment.
Follow up
  • The cancer needs to be followed closely.
Time consumption
  • Patient need to go for blood tests and biopsies more frequently (in case of active surveillance)
Cancer may spread
  • The cancer may spread and become harder to treat. However, the chance is low if the prostate cancer risk of progression is low.
