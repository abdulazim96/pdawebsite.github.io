What do I need to know about surgery ?

  • Surgery is a treatment choice for men with earlystage prostate cancer who are in good health.
  • You will be admitted to the hospital for one or more days.
  • The entire prostate gland with the cancer in it will be removed during surgery.
  • Sometimes, the doctor will also remove the lymph glands (nodes) next to the prostate.
  • The surgery usually takes about 2 to 4 hours.
  • The surgery can be performed in several ways :
    • Open prostatectomy
    • Laparoscopic / robotic surgery

Advantages

  • Surgery can offer a cure if the cancer is removed completely.

Disadvantages

  • Sometimes not all cancer cells are removed.
  • Complications of the surgery :
    • Bleeding
    • Blood clots
    • Infection
